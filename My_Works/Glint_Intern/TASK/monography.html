Natural Houses      THE RESIDENTIAL ARCHITECTURE OF ANDERSSON-WISE


Natural Houses presents seven of the Austin, Texas-based firm’s exquisitely crafted projects. Precise and cool, these are painstakingly crafted houses made from regionally appropriate and aesthetically timeless materials.






Natural Houses presents a range of sites and residences – from a small cabin in the woods to a multibuilding camp. Exceptional photography captures the light and atmosphere of each project setting and illustrates how the firm rigorously expresses the design concept through detailing and construction. An introduction by Rick Sundberg and essays by Jen Renzi and Frederick Steiner chart the firm’s evolution and influences.
